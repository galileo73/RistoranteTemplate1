# Commercial Strategy: RistoranteTemplate

**Positioning:** White-label restaurant website template for small-to-medium restaurants, cafés, bars, and local food businesses.

---

## 1. Ideal Target Clients

### Primary Market (80% of revenue)

| Segment | Profile | Budget | Timeline |
|---------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Independent Restaurants** | Family-owned, 20-80 seats, local focus | $500-2,000 | 1-2 weeks |
| **New Restaurant Openings** | Just launching, need quick online presence | $1,000-3,000 | 2-4 weeks |
| **Cafés & Coffee Shops** | Artisan coffee, local baked goods | $300-1,000 | 1 week |
| **Wine Bars & Gastropubs** | Upscale casual, focus on drinks/food pairing | $800-2,500 | 2-3 weeks |
| **Food Trucks & Pop-ups** | Mobile, need flexible booking/contact | $300-800 | 1 week |

### Secondary Market (20% of revenue)

| Segment | Profile | Budget | Timeline |
|---------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Restaurant Chains** | 3-10 locations, need consistency | $3,000-8,000 | 4-8 weeks |
| **Hotels with Restaurants** | Need integrated booking/dining | $2,000-5,000 | 3-6 weeks |
| **Event Venues** | Catering, private dining rooms | $1,500-4,000 | 2-4 weeks |
| **Food Delivery Ghost Kitchens** | Online ordering focus | $800-2,000 | 1-2 weeks |

### Target Client Profile

**Ideal Client:**
- Annual revenue: $200K - $2M
- No dedicated IT staff
- Existing website outdated or missing
- Needs multi-language support (tourism areas)
- Value conscious but quality focused
- Owner-operated or small management team

**Red Flags (Avoid):**
- Budget under $300 (not serious)
- Wants extensive custom features immediately
- No clear decision maker
- Expects 24/7 support for $500 one-time fee

**Checklist:**
```
[ ] Create client folder in workspace
[ ] Copy template to client folder
[ ] Update restaurant.js with intake data
[ ] Update menu.js with menu items
[ ] Update backend/config/menu.py
[ ] Replace placeholder images
[ ] Replace placeholders in public files
[ ] Run build verification
[ ] Deploy to staging
```

### Phase 3: Review (Day 3)

**Staging Link Delivery:**
- Send preview URL
- Request feedback via form
- Limit to 2 revision rounds
- Collect final approval

### Phase 4: Launch (Day 4-5)

**Launch Checklist:**
```
[ ] Final content approval
[ ] Domain connected
[ ] SSL verified
[ ] Google Analytics installed
[ ] Google Search Console submitted
[ ] Reservation system tested
[ ] Contact form tested
[ ] Mobile responsiveness verified
[ ] Social links verified
```

## 8. Competitive Positioning

### Competitors Analysis

| Competitor | Price | Weakness | Our Advantage |
|------------|-------|----------|---------------|
| Squarespace | $23-65/mo | Generic templates, restaurant-specific features limited | Restaurant-specific, multi-language |
| Wix Restaurants | $26-45/mo | Heavy, slow, locked in | Lightweight, fast, own hosting |
| WordPress themes | $50-100 one-time | Requires setup, plugins, maintenance | No plugins needed, modern stack |
| Toast | $165+/mo | Expensive, locked in | Own hosting, lower cost |
| Restaurant-specific themes | $50-200 one-time | Limited support, outdated tech | Modern React, actively maintained |

### Value Proposition

**For Price-Sensitive Clients:**
> "Professional restaurant website for less than your monthly marketing budget. One-time payment, no subscription lock-in."

**For Quality-Focused Clients:**
> "Modern, fast, mobile-first design that ranks well on Google. Built with the same technology as major restaurant chains."

**For Speed-Focused Clients:**
> "Live in 5 days with everything configured. Just provide your content and we handle the rest."

### Differentiation

1. **No Vendor Lock-in** - Host anywhere, own your code
2. **One-Time Payment** - No monthly fees for core features
3. **Multi-Language Built-in** - Perfect for tourist areas
4. **Modern Tech Stack** - Fast, accessible, SEO-optimized
5. **Transparent Pricing** - No hidden costs, clear packages
6. **White-Label Ready** - Perfect for agencies to resell

### Positioning Statement

> "RistoranteTemplate is a professional restaurant website template that delivers enterprise-quality features at small business prices. Built with modern technology, designed for conversion, and priced for value-conscious restaurant owners who want to own their online presence without ongoing fees."
